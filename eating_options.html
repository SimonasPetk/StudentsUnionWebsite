Check out the places where to eat!

The View Deli

Meat is OFF the menu here against the fantastic city backdrop, tuck into a host of freshly made vegetarian, vegan and gluten free treats and tasty lunches - don’t forget to take a look at the daily chalkboard menu and view our range of Coffee HoUSe hot and cold drinks.

Opening Times:
Mon – Fri: 8.30am – 4pm
Sat/Sun: Closed
Follow us on Twitter @ViewDeli

UNI Central

Come and meet the team inside the Students’ Union, preparing an array of exciting food from the Stack It burger, Dough pizza and Grill & Go wrap stations.

Opening Times:
Mon – Fri: 11.30am – 2.30pm
Sat/Sun: Closed
Follow us on Twitter @UniCentraL4

Grill and Go

It is the perfect place for burritos, hot wraps and salad boxes, cooked fresh and made to order. They are healthy and packed full of flavour – perfect for those on the go

Opening Times:
Mon – Fri: 11am – 4.30pm
Sat/Sun: Closed
Follow us on Twitter @grill_go

Inox

It is a flexible event space located on the fifth floor of the University of Sheffield Students' Union Building. We provide all day dining, venue hire and a place for meetings and social working. Book a table today or drop in and see Inox for yourself

Opening Times:
Mon – Fri: 11.30am – 2.30pm
Sat/Sun: Closed
Follow us on Twitter @InoxSheffield

New Leaf

If you are looking for something healthy to eat, this place has got just the thing. They have fresh salads and wraps with a ton of choices of vegetables in it. The combinations are endless! Located on the ground floor of the Students Union it really is very convinient to grab a bite on the way to the classes

Opening Times:
Mon – Fri: 11:00am – 3.30pm
Sat/Sun: Closed
Follow us on Twitter@NewLeafSalad

Coffee Revolution

This place is the perfect place for a quick meet with friends over a cup of coffee or over some cakes and pastries because they have it all. They serve from a wide range of teas, smoothies, pastries, sandwiches and brownies. One thing to keep in mind if you are going to visit this place often is to take your own Coffee cup because they actively encourage the use of reusable cups to reduce disposables, and also reward customers for doing so too.

Opening Times:
Mon – Thu: 7.30am – 9pm
Fri: 7:30am - 6pm and 7pm - 10pm (Mocktail lounge)
Sat: 10am - 6pm and 7pm - 9pm (Reclaim the night)
12pm - 6pm
Follow us on Twitter @CoffeeRevSu
