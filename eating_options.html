Come and meet the team inside the Students’ Union, at UNI Central preparing an array of exciting food from the Stack It burger, Dough pizza and Grill & Go wrap stations.

Opening Times:
Mon – Fri: 11.30am – 2.30pm
Sat/Sun: Closed
Follow us on Twitter @UniCentraL4

Located inside the Students’ Union, Grill and Go is the perfect place for burritos, hot wraps and salad boxes, cooked fresh and made to order. They are healthy and packed full of flavour – perfect for those on the go

Opening Times:
Mon – Fri: 11am – 4.30pm
Sat/Sun: Closed
Follow us on Twitter @grill_go

Inox is a flexible event space located on the fifth floor of the University of Sheffield Students' Union Building. We provide all day dining, venue hire and a place for meetings and social working. Book a table today or drop in and see Inox for yourself

Follow us on Twitter @InoxSheffield
